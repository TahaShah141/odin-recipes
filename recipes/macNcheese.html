Mac and Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • A Box of Macoroni
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika Powder
  • Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Boil the Macoroni in salted water
  2. Drain and transfer the Macoroni into a baking dish
  3. Melt Butter and whisk in the flour
  4. Whisk in the milk, bring it to a simmer
  5. Stir in the cheese
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Continue simmering until the sauce is thick
  8. Pour the sauce over the Macoroni
  9. Melt two table spoons of butter in a skillet
  10. Add bread crumbs to it and toast until brown
  11. Spread the crumbs over the mac and cheese
  12. Sprinkle paprika all over
  13. Bake until golden brown
  14. Enjoy.
