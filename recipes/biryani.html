Biryani

Description

Ingredients

  • Chicken (1kg)
  • Rice (1kg)
  • Onion (500g)
  • Tomato (500g)
  • Green Chillies (5-6 units)
  • Ginger paste (1 tbsp)
  • Garlic paste (1 tsp)
  • Biryani Masala (3 tbsp)
  • Oil (1/2 cup)

Steps

  1. Boil the white rice and keep aside
  2. Fry Onions in oil till golden brown on medium heat
  3. Put Ginger and Garlic pastes and Tomatoes (cut) with the Onions
  4. Stir well
  5. Add Chicken
  6. Add the Biryani Masala
  7. Fry till it dries up
  8. Add 1/2 cup water to the pan
  9. Cover the pot with low flame for 15 minutes (till the meat is tender)
  10. Put it on high flame till it starts bubbling
  11. Take another pot
  12. Add the boiled rice to it and the chicken curry, be sure to layer them one by one in portions
  13. Cover the pot on a low flame for 10 mins
  14. Stir well
  15. Enjoy.
