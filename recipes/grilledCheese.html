Grilled Cheese

Description

Ingredients

  • White Bread
  • Butter
  • Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat a non-stick pan on medium heat
  2. Butter one side of two pieces of bread
  3. Place the bread pieces, butter side down onto the pan
  4. Add cheese on top of one of the bread
  5. Cook until brown on one side
  6. Flip the bread and cook until the cheese melts
  7. Combine the two breads with the buttered side being on the outside
  8. Enjoy.
